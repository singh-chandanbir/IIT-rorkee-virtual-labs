To Determine Resonance Frequency in a series RLC circuit

Instructions

  • !!! Click on labels to delete connectinos for the corrusponding node.
  • Make connections as per the instructions given below.
    • 1->13, 2->14
    • 3->15, 4->16
    • 5->15, 9->16
    • 6->7, 8->12, 10->11
  • Now, Check the connections by clicking on ‘CHECK’ button.
    • If the connections are ‘Invalid connections’ click on corresponding node to remove the connection.
    • And if the connections are ‘Right Connections’ then follow the below steps.
  • Turn on the MCB.
  • Click on Function Generator ‘Power’ button.
  • Vary the frequency by moving the slider.
  • Click on ‘ADD’ button to add the readings in the observation table.
  • Repeat the above steps to get atleast 6 observations.
  • Now manually input the values in caluclation tab.
