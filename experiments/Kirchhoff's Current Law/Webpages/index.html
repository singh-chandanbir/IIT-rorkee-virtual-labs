Instructions

  • !!! Click on the label to delete all connections for the corresponding node.
  • Make connections by dragging nodes from aparatus to the circuit.
    • Connect power-supply to the right most nodes of the curcit. (1->9, 2->10)
    • Connect ammeters (3-11, 4-12) and (5-13, 6-14) and (7-15, 8-16) or you can connect any ammeter to any resistance in circuit.
    • Click on the label to delete all the connections for the corresponding nodes.
  • Check your connections by clicking on the 'CHECK' button.
  • Set the values of resistances R1, R2, R3 by adjusting the sliders on left, these values will remain constant throughout the experiment.
  • Turn on the Power Supply and choose a voltage value, the reading on ammeters will change accordingly.
  • Click on the 'ADD' button to add the values to observation table.
  • Add a few observations to the table by while changing the value of voltage from Power Supply.
  • Click on the 'PLOT' button to create a plot.
  • Click on 'RESET' button to refresh the page.
